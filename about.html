About US

At Prime Hospitals in Kakinada, we believe excellent healthcare is a right, not a privilege. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality healthcare to our patients and strive to make our services accessible and affordable to everyone in our community. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and facilities, and our team of skilled medical professionals is committed to delivering personalized, compassionate care to every one of our patients. We are proud to have a team of highly skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals. Our physicians are trained in various medical specialties and are dedicated to delivering personalized, expert care to our patients. Our nurses are compassionate and committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. We also have a team of support staff, including administrators, financial counselors, and patient educators, who are dedicated to ensuring that our patients and their families have the resources they need to, make informed healthcare decisions. Together, we work as a team to deliver the highest quality healthcare to our patients and their families.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced medical equipment allow us to provide comprehensive care for various health concerns. We offer a range of medical services, including consultations with specialist physicians, orthopedic care, dermatology and cosmetology treatments, gynecology services, and sports medicine consultations. We partner with local organizations to provide vital healthcare services to underserved populations and offer volunteer opportunities for individuals who want to make a difference. We are also proud to support local events and initiatives and to engage with our community through social media and other channels. We believe that we have to be responsible and active members of the community, and we are committed to doing our part in the lives of those around us.
